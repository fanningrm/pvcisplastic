Ventura County Star: Calleguas water district, Oxnard, Ventura on prevailing side of faulty pipe lawsuit

On six occasions from 1995 to 2009, a stretch of pipe Calleguas Municipal Water District had purchased from JM Eagle had major breaks under Kanan Road in Oak Park.

Posted by PVC is Plastic on November 15, 2013

On six occasions from 1995 to 2009, a stretch of pipe Calleguas Municipal Water District had purchased from JM Eagle had major breaks under Kanan Road in Oak Park.

The district originally paid $2.5 million for all of the piping and just under $5 million to repair the bursting portion, General Manager Susan Mulligan said Friday.

The district, along with Oxnard and Ventura, were among 42 water agencies and cities to join Nevada, New Mexico and Virginia in a whistle-blower lawsuit that began when a former JM Eagle employee said the company knowingly made and sold substandard plastic pipes to government agencies.
